Chasing The Tangents

Exploring My Thinking Style

September 09, 2015

So phase-0 has been everything I've expected it to be and more. I've been inspired, motivated, challenged and utterly defeated. I've found myself humbled by my peers and everything that we've been able to learn from one another. Frankly, I'm humbled by just how much I don't know... That isn't to say that I'm not enjoying the experience, on the contrary, in today's day and age it's difficult to find this kind of stimuli. I would even go as far as to say that my biggest struggle is in processing all the information that's been thrown at me. I go off on tangents about 90% of the time, no really that's not an exaggeration, but that's because for the first time in a long time I've found myself in an environment conducive to my style of thinking.
